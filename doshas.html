Ayurveda - Kaph, Pitta, and Vata Doshas

Learn about the three primary doshas: Kaph, Pitta, and Vata. Discover their symptoms, remedies, and more!

Kaph Dosha

Symptoms of Kaph Dosha:

  • Slow digestion
  • Weight gain
  • Excessive sleep
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Stubbornness

Remedies for Kaph Dosha:

  • Exercise regularly
  • Consume warm, spicy foods
  • Avoid dairy and heavy foods
  • Practice yoga to balance energy

Pitta Dosha

Symptoms of Pitta Dosha:

  • Acid reflux or heartburn
  • Increased body heat
  • Red or irritated skin
  • Strong appetite
  • Anger or irritability

Remedies for Pitta Dosha:

  • Consume cooling foods
  • Avoid spicy and oily foods
  • Practice relaxation techniques
  • Stay hydrated

Vata Dosha

Symptoms of Vata Dosha:

  • Dry skin
  • Anxiety or nervousness
  • Irregular sleep patterns
  • Thin body frame
  • Excessive gas and bloating

Remedies for Vata Dosha:

  • Drink warm liquids
  • Eat grounding foods (e.g., root vegetables)
  • Avoid cold, dry environments
  • Practice calming meditation
